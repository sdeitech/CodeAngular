Module - Chart of Accounts  

Used to Add/Edit accounts associated with the organisation. 
Accounts can be Bank Accounts, Income Accounts, Expense Accounts, Asset Accounts or Liability Accounts. 

Chart of Accounts is a list of the account names a company uses to label transactions and keep tabs on its finances. 
You use a Chart of Accounts to organize transactions into groups, which in turn helps you track money coming in and out of the company.
